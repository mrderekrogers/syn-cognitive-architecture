## About this repository

This is a public, engineering-forward overview of Syn, a self-built multi-agent LLM cognitive architecture. It documents the design and the reasoning behind it.

The full source, deployment configuration, internal schemas, and a live code walkthrough are kept private and are available on request. If you are evaluating this work or want to go deeper on any subsystem, reach out and I am happy to walk through it.
